WHAT IS AI DEV CON?
As the name suggests, it's a conference-plus-expo for techies and developers who want to stay ahead of the race by either "working with AI" or "working on AI".
WHO ATTENDS?
Developers
DevOps, DevSecOps and ML-Ops Professionals
Data Management Professionals
IT Infra Managers
CXOs: Sr Tech Decision Makers
Academicians & Researchers
DIYers & Tech Enthusiasts
Tech Media, Bloggers & Influencers
WHY ATTEND?
If you don't want to get obsolete, and want to stay up-to-date with the latest AI-powered tools and techniques related to development of software and tech, then you must invest a day or two in attending this conference. Plus, you can also figure out how you can contribute towards developing pathbreaking AI frameworks and platforms like ChatGPT and DeepSeek. Meet the brightest minds in this field, listen to experts, get up to date, so you're in full control of your destiny and that of your teams.
